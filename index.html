Our Square Quartz Watch comes with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty covering any defects in materials or workmanship. The warranty does not cover damage caused by accidents, misuse, or normal wear and tear.

The Japanese quartz movement in our watch is highly accurate, typically losing or gaining only about 15 seconds per month. This is significantly more accurate than mechanical watches and requires minimal maintenance.

The watch case is made from high-quality alloy with a durable plating that resists tarnishing. The gold and silver finishes are applied using advanced electroplating techniques to ensure long-lasting color and shine.

The battery in your Square Quartz Watch typically lasts between 18 to 24 months, depending on usage. When the battery needs replacement, we recommend having it done by a professional jeweler or watch repair service.
